About Me

I am a dedicated and adaptable IT professional with a strong foundation in operational security, project management, and multilingual communication. Currently pursuing a B.A. in Interdepartmental Studies—Business Studies Track with a Technology and Inquiry emphasis at the University of Iowa, along with minors in Computer Science and Business.
