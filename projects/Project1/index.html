About Us

Welcome to the University of Iowa IT Rooms directory. As the Physical Infrastructure Team, our goal is to maintain and enhance the university's IT facilities to ensure optimal performance and accessibility. We are committed to providing a secure, efficient, and innovative IT environment that supports the academic and administrative functions of the university. Our vision is to set the standard for IT infrastructure excellence within the Big Ten universities, continually improving and adapting to meet the needs of our diverse campus community.

About the Site

This demo version of the University of Iowa IT Rooms directory provides detailed information and directions to all campus IT rooms. Certain information is omitted for security reasons to ensure the safety and integrity of our infrastructure. Displayed on my personal website, this demo showcases the directory's capabilities and functionalities. It is not the official site and is intended for demonstration purposes only. This resource aims to help students, faculty, and visitors navigate our state-of-the-art IT environments.
