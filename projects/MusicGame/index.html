Welcome to MusicGame

What is MusicGame?

MusicGame is a unique combination of rhythm-based gameplay and platformer mechanics. The challenge is to navigate through levels while maintaining the beat, creating an engaging and rhythmic gaming experience.

Objective

The goal of MusicGame is to collect musical notes and avoid obstacles while keeping in time with the music. Higher scores are achieved by maintaining combos and perfectly timing jumps.

Scoring

  • Points are earned by collecting musical notes.
  • Avoiding obstacles successfully contributes to the score.
  • Maintaining combos and timing jumps perfectly can lead to higher scores.

Power-ups

Various power-ups are available to enhance gameplay and provide strategic advantages, including:

  • Speed boosts
  • Invincibility
  • Double points

Game Mechanics

The main mechanics of the game include:

  • Background: Sets the scene for the game.
  • Control: Player control mechanics.
  • Intersection: Manages interactions between game elements.
  • MusicGame: Core game logic.
  • Paddle: Player's movement mechanism.
  • PowerUp: Manages power-up mechanics.
  • Player: The player's character.
  • Sound: Music and sound effects integration.

Bugs and Gameplay Issues

During development, several bugs and gameplay issues were encountered:

  • Power-up spawns
  • Game progression

How to Play

To play MusicGame, extract the Music-Game-main.zip file and open the index.html file in a web browser. Use the arrow keys to navigate and the spacebar to jump. Collect musical notes and avoid obstacles to earn points and power-ups.
